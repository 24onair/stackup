About CHIP! CHIP!

A one-tap, real-physics color-stacking game you can play free in any browser.

CHIP! CHIP! 칩칩! is a hyper-casual stacking game with a simple promise: one tap, real physics, and a tower of color that leans, wobbles, and eventually crashes. There are no menus to learn and no second button to press. A Pantone-inspired color chip swings above your tower; you tap to drop it; and a genuine physics simulation decides whether it settles or sends everything tumbling. It is easy to start and surprisingly hard to master.

The game is built around a single idea — that a game with exactly one control can still be deep, tense, and beautiful. The tension comes from real rigid-body physics: every chip has weight, friction, and a center of mass, so a tall tower is a living, precarious thing rather than a scripted animation. The beauty comes from a warm, print-inspired art style and eight hand-made pixel-art cities to climb.

Eight cities to stack in

Because the only control is a tap, the backgrounds carry the sense of place and progress. Pick a city and climb its skyline from the street all the way to the stratosphere. Each city has its own signature color and landmark set, drawn as pixel art:

  • Seoul — Gwanghwamun's gate, the Han River bridges, N Seoul Tower and the city skyline at dusk.
  • Mungyeong — the mountain-pass gate of Mungyeongsaejae, pine ridges, apple trees and drying persimmons.
  • Queenstown — the Southern Alps, a lake steamer, and New Zealand's dramatic mountain skyline.
  • Tokyo — pagoda rooftops, neon towers, and a dense metropolitan sprawl.
  • Paris — the Eiffel Tower, the Arc de Triomphe, and rose-grey rooftops.
  • London — Big Ben, bridges over the Thames, and a grey-gold skyline.
  • New York — skyscrapers, water towers, and the classic Manhattan grid.
  • Cairo — desert light, the pyramids and the Sphinx on the horizon.

Above every city, the top zone opens into a shared night sky — clouds, hot-air balloons, then stars, a crescent moon, and a tiny satellite as you break into the stratosphere.

Design language

The look is deliberately un-glossy: a warm paper background, dark ink outlines, and hard offset shadows with no blur — a printed, sticker-like feel. The chips themselves are the signature object: a color face over a cream label printed with a color code, like a paint swatch. One warm orange is reserved for the single most important action on any screen, so the interface never shouts.

Sound

The audio is intentionally tactile — paper-and-wood foley for landings instead of harsh electronic beeps, a relaxed lounge soundtrack, and a soft wind ambience that thins out as you climb into the sky. Perfect landings ring a little higher with every combo.

Technology

  • Pure HTML5, Canvas, and vanilla JavaScript — no heavy framework, fast to load.
  • Rigid-body physics powered by the Matter.js engine.
  • A global leaderboard with unique, first-come nicknames.
  • Installable as a Progressive Web App (PWA) — add it to your home screen and play offline-friendly.
  • English and Korean, with automatic language detection.

Free to play

CHIP! CHIP! is free. It runs on phones, tablets, and desktops, right in the browser — nothing to install. Play it at playchipchip.com.

CHIP! CHIP! 칩칩!은 단순한 약속을 담은 하이퍼캐주얼 스태킹 게임입니다 — 한 번의 터치, 진짜 물리, 그리고 기울고 흔들리다 결국 무너지는 컬러의 탑. 배울 메뉴도, 두 번째 버튼도 없습니다. 팬톤 컬러칩이 탑 위에서 스윙하고, 터치하면 떨어지며, 진짜 물리 시뮬레이션이 안착할지 와르르 무너질지를 결정합니다. 시작은 쉽지만 마스터는 의외로 어렵습니다.

이 게임은 하나의 발상 위에 서 있습니다 — 조작이 딱 하나뿐인 게임도 깊고, 긴장감 있고, 아름다울 수 있다는 것. 긴장감은 실제 강체 물리에서 나옵니다. 모든 칩에 무게·마찰·무게중심이 있어, 높은 탑은 스크립트된 애니메이션이 아니라 살아 있는 아슬아슬한 구조물입니다. 아름다움은 따뜻한 인쇄풍 아트와 손으로 만든 8개 픽셀아트 도시에서 나옵니다.

디자인 언어

룩은 일부러 매끈하지 않게 — 따뜻한 종이 배경, 짙은 잉크 외곽선, 블러 없는 하드 오프셋 섀도로 인쇄물·스티커 같은 질감을 냅니다. 칩 자체가 시그니처 오브젝트로, 크림색 라벨 위에 컬러 코드가 찍힌 페인트 스와치 같은 모양입니다. 화면마다 가장 중요한 액션 한 곳에만 따뜻한 오렌지를 써서 인터페이스가 소란스럽지 않습니다.

사운드

오디오는 의도적으로 촉각적입니다 — 전자음 대신 종이·나무 폴리 착지음, 여유로운 라운지 사운드트랙, 고도가 오를수록 옅어지는 바람 앰비언스. 퍼펙트 착지는 콤보마다 조금씩 높은 음으로 울립니다.
